# Taste Ledger

Persistent record of design judgements, aesthetic decisions, and taste signals observed across projects. Survives sessions and projects via global location.

## Entries

## Calibration

User-expressed taste verdicts recorded during onboarding (and updated incrementally over time).
Each entry captures a design pattern the user reacted to, their verdict, and — critically — the
reason behind it. New entries append; nothing is overwritten.

Entry format:

```
- pattern: <short label, e.g. "editorial-minimal landing page">
  verdict: love | hate
  why: <one or two sentences — the specific quality that triggered the reaction>
  date: YYYY-MM-DD
```
